Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q:How big will the cake be?

    Ans)You may order the cake based on size or based on actual guest count.Cakes are charged based on SIZE+DECOR LABOR

  • Q:How do you determine the price of cake?

    Ans)Cakes are typically priced by the amount of servings they feed + the work required for the design.The more the detail or time required for the working cost,the more it will cost

  • Q:We are on a budget:Can you help?

    Ans)We do our best to work within financial constraints,but please inform your ideas to us so that we can plan based on it,as pricing is based more on working hours than on cake ingredients.

  • Q:We are not clear which flavour of cake we want;can you help us with some new and different flavour?

    Ans)Our pleasure,that's what we do.Please submit an inquiry to our email id mentioned in our site and we will get in touch with you for further discussion.

  • Q:How do we reach you?What information do I need to provide to order a cake?

    Ans)You can reach out to us through social media and the links are embedded in the footer section of our site and then we can discuss about the orders
